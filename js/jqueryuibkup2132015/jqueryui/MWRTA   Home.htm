Up To The Minute Route Info

MWRTA is participating in Google Transit - click here and then click "Get Directions" to utilize this service. Use the Google Transit Trip Planner to get step by step transit directions in your web browser. Enter a starting and ending address or destination serviced by MWRTA and you will be given schedule and fare information for navigating the bus system.

The Google Transit Trip Planner is also available for some mobile phones by navigating to http://m.google.com/maps in your mobile browser. You may also get directions to and from the MBTA service area into and out of the MWRTA service area.
